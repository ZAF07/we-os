# Rubric — Creative brief

- Expresses the approved **positioning and messaging** — invents no new strategy.
- Contains: creative concept(s), campaign theme(s), content direction(s), and
  explicit **asset requirements** (what asset, which channel, to what spec).
- Each concept ties back to the business objective and to a messaging point.
- Asset requirements are specific enough that a prompt-writer can act on them
  without guessing (format, channel, aspect/length, key message, mandatory
  elements).
- Honors DNA brand voice and hard constraints.
- **Briefs only** — contains no finished copy and no image/video/ad/landing-page
  generation prompts.
