# Rubric — Performance plan

- **Channel selection** is justified: each chosen channel has a rationale tied to
  the audience/segment and the business objective; obvious non-fits are excluded
  with a reason.
- Includes campaign setup recommendations concrete enough to execute (targeting,
  structure, formats matched to the creative brief's assets).
- Defines the **KPI plan across all three tiers** (Business / Marketing /
  Creative) with concrete targets, and **defines success metrics before
  recommending spend.**
- **Budget allocation** sums to the campaign budget and is justified per channel.
- Optimization guidance is **data-driven** (what to measure, what thresholds
  trigger what action) — not assumption.
- Every recommendation explains why and ties back to the business KPI.
- **No creative or generation prompts.**
