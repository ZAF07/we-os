# Rubric — Campaign strategy (Marketing Director)

- States the campaign **approach** and how it serves the business objective.
- Names the **channels at a glance** with a one-line rationale each (full channel
  detail is the performance plan's job, but the shortlist + reasoning belongs
  here).
- Includes a **budget allocation** consistent with the campaign budget in the
  goal.
- Defines **all three KPI tiers** — Business, Marketing, Creative — with concrete
  targets, and shows how creative success ladders up to marketing success ladders
  up to the business KPI.
- Builds on the approved brand strategy; does not contradict or re-open
  positioning/messaging.
- **No creative execution and no generation prompts.**
- Every choice explains its why and ties to the business objective.
