# Rubric — Market research findings

- Covers all five lenses: customer, competitor, market, trends, audience
  segmentation. A missing lens is a gap that must be named, not skipped silently.
- Each finding **cites its source or the framework** it came from. Unsourced
  assertions are not findings.
- Customer findings name the real jobs-to-be-done and pain points from the DNA —
  not invented personas.
- Competitor findings name actual competitors (from the DNA where available) and
  how they position, not generic "competition is strong."
- Segmentation is actionable: segments are distinguishable and sized/qualified
  well enough for a strategist to choose between them.
- **Findings only.** No positioning, messaging, creative, or channel/budget
  recommendations — those belong to later stages.
- Gaps and unknowns are flagged honestly with what evidence would resolve them.
