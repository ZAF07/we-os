# Rubric — Brand strategy

- Contains all four: **positioning, messaging, brand personality/voice, value
  proposition.** None omitted.
- Positioning states a distinct, defensible place versus the named competitors —
  not "we're the best." It is a choice, with trade-offs.
- The core message and supporting points each trace to a specific research
  finding (cite it). Strategy does not override or ignore the research.
- Value proposition is framed around the customer's jobs-to-be-done / pain points
  from the DNA, in their language.
- Brand voice is specific enough to write to (concrete do's and don'ts), honoring
  any DNA voice guardrails.
- **Strategy only.** No creative concepts, taglines-as-assets, channel plans, or
  generation prompts.
- Every recommendation explains why and ties to the business objective.
