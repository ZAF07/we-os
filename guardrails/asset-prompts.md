# Rubric — Asset generation prompts

- **Every prompt traces to a specific brief requirement** and is labelled with the
  requirement it fulfills. No prompt exists that the brief did not ask for.
- Invents no new strategy, positioning, messaging, or creative direction — strictly
  executes the brief.
- Prompts are concrete and complete for their medium (image / video / ad /
  landing page): subject, style, format/spec, mandatory brand elements, and the
  message to land.
- Honors DNA brand voice and hard constraints (off-limits claims, brand no-gos).
- If the brief is ambiguous or incomplete, the deliverable says so and stops on
  that item rather than filling the gap with invention.
