# Operating Principles

Marketing OS is a **decision-making system, not a content generator**. The goal is always revenue growth; content and assets are downstream tools, never the objective.

## The 8 non-negotiable rules
1. Strategy before content.
2. Revenue before engagement.
3. Customer understanding before campaign planning.
4. Campaign goals before asset generation.
5. Recommendations must be evidence-based.
6. Every recommendation must explain **why**.
7. Every recommendation must tie back to a business objective.
8. Agents cannot bypass upstream decisions.

## KPI tiers — every campaign defines all three
- **Business KPI** — revenue, leads, bookings, sales, repeat purchases, retention.
- **Marketing KPI** — CTR, CPC, CPM, conversion rate.
- **Creative KPI** — hook rate, watch time, engagement rate.

See `decision-hierarchy.md` for the mandatory pipeline these principles govern.
