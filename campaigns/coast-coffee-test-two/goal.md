# Campaign Goal — Coast Coffee Launch

> Customer DNA (`customers/coast-coffee/dna.md`) is complete. This goal is finalised; KPI
> targets are evidence-based proposals by the Marketing Director — adjust if the customer

## Required

- **Customer:** coast-coffee
- **Primary business objective:** Acquire **70 new customers** in 8 weeks (first-time buyers of Coast Coffee).
- **Timeframe:** 8 weeks from launch.
- **Campaign budget:** SGD 1,000 total paid spend across the run (~SGD 125/week).
- **Target segment for this campaign:** Singapore home baristas who want curated, guaranteed-fresh Asian-origin beans matched to their flavour preference (from DNA).

### Success metrics (define all three tiers)

- **Business KPI:** 70 new customers in 8 weeks → implied **blended CAC ≤ SGD 14** (paid SGD 1,000 expected to drive ~35–45 of the 70; remainder from organic). Secondary: AOV ≥ SGD 50 via the trio offer.
- **Marketing KPI:** Landing-page **conversion rate ≥ 3%**; **cost per purchase ≤ SGD 25** on paid channels; CTR ≥ 1.0% on paid social.
- **Creative KPI:** **Hook rate ≥ 25%** (3-sec/thumb-stop) on the lead freshness/origin video; engagement rate ≥ 4% on organic posts.

## Optional

- **Offer / promotion (proposed — validate):** an introductory **3-bean flavour trio** — one from each tier (safe / complex-floral / exciting-unknown) — as a low-risk discovery sampler that showcases the curation system. Target trio price ~SGD 50–54 (light bundle value vs. SGD 18–30/bag), confirm economics.
- **Campaign-specific constraints:** inherit the DNA hard constraints — at most 3 flavour variations, SGD 18–30 per 200–250 g bag, and the ≥6-week freshness promise (don't overstate).
