## Required (the agent will not start without these)

### Business
- **Business name:** Coast Coffee
- **What they sell:** Specialty coffee beans sourced from Asian regions (China, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, etc.), sold by the bag (200–250 g) — tested and vacuum-sealed after degassing. Organised under a curated 3-tier flavour system.
- **Category / industry:** Direct-to-consumer specialty coffee beans; mail-order to home baristas.
- **Price point:** SGD 18–30 per 200/250 g bag.

### Customers
- **Primary segment(s):** Singapore home baristas who brew specialty coffee at home and want beans matched to their taste. Includes those who prefer beans **delivered** rather than travelling to buy.
- **Pain points / jobs-to-be-done:**
  - Want variety of bean profiles matched to their preference (from safe to adventurous).
  - Want guaranteed-fresh beans — properly degassed, vacuum-sealed, long shelf life.
  - Want the convenience of home delivery.
  - Want to discover interesting, less-common Asian-origin beans.

### Differentiation
- **Why customers choose them over alternatives:**
  1. A curated **3-tier flavour framework** that matches adventurousness — *Tier 1 basic & safe, Tier 2 complex & floral, Tier 3 exciting & unknown* — so home baristas pick by preference, not guesswork.
  2. **Tested beans, vacuum-sealed after degassing, with guaranteed freshness ≥6 weeks** — freshness they can rely on by mail.
  3. Focus on **Asian-origin** beans (China, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia…), less common than typical African/Latin American origins.
  4. **Home delivery** for baristas who can't always travel to buy.
  5. Beans matched to **individual barista profiles/preferences**.
